AI-Powered Features

Two AIs.
One App.

The first reloading app to combine computer vision and language AI. Not chatbots — purpose-built tools for competitive shooters.

01 📸 ⭐ Premium · Gemini Vision

AI Target Analysis

Photograph any paper target. PrecisionLoad measures group size in MOA and inches, identifies your point of impact vs. point of aim, detects flyers, and gives you specific adjustment recommendations.

02 🧠 ⭐ Premium · Claude AI

AI Load Development

Feed in your velocity data, SD, and ES. Claude AI analyzes your load ladder, identifies trends, and suggests your next powder charge, seating depth adjustment, or component change.

03 📡 ⭐ Premium · LabRadar

Chronograph Import

Import LabRadar CSV files directly. MV, SD, and ES calculate automatically from your shot string — no manual entry, no transcription errors.
